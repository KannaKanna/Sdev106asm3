King by Kanaria, Covered by Kuraiinu Ft The Musical Ghost

The Original

Posted by Kanaria on Aug 2, 2020, King by Kanaria was a sensational hit online, originally being posted on NicoNicoDouga, a japanese streaming website before being uploaded on youtube. This song was highly popular online as a cover song due to its nature of being produced with a vocal synthysizer, aka a vocaloid, and thus was covered by several musicans online, especially those who made a career online as a singer

Themes

The song aswell as its squel song, Queen, Primarily talks about the toxic love between two individuals, with King focusing on a partner who gaslights their partner into thinking they are the ones in control despite the power dynamic saying otherwise

The Cover

Kuraiinu, a singer who made a career of covering and later releasing his own original songs, covered the song in the following year on Jun 26, 2021, with his own unique twist of modifying the beat of the song with the help of The Musical Ghost to turn the song into a Electroswing remix, whilst translating the lyrics from Japanese to English
